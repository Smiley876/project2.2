What is Cybersecurity and Why is it important?

What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is all about safeguarding systems, networks, and data from digital attacks. Think of it as a set of practices and tools designed to protect your information from being stolen, corrupted, or accessed by unauthorized users.

Why is it important?

Cybersecurity is crucial for: Protection of Sensitive Data, Business Continuity, Preventing Financial Loss, Safeguarding Identity, Compliance with Regulations, National Security and Preventing Cyber Attacks.
